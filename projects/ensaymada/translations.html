/translations

please excuse my tagalog — it is almost non-understandable! i am still relearning...


nagtrabaho mo po ngayon higit sa dapat mong magtrabaho? / have you worked more than you should have today?

bunso / youngest child

mukang pagod mo. katulad ng dati. / you look tired. as usual.

masyedong / too much

isip mo po ng iba? takot ka magtanong sa akin? / do you think of other things? are you scared to ask me?

kahit na magtanong mo po iyan magpakailanman, hindi iyon magayusin ako. / even if you’re going to ask me that forever, [that constant questioning] won’t fix me.

ikaw ay dapat magalala ako. ikaw ang aking nanay. / you should care/worry about me. you’re my mother.

hindi kailangan ng mahal maging pamimintas o pangamba. / love doesn’t need to be criticism or fear.

parating sakit yung tiyan mo. baka pagkakasakit ikaw ... / your stomach always hurts. you might have an illness …

ikaw ang hindi alam. / you’re the one that doesn’t know.

bulok / rotten/expired

mas maraming bulok sa bahay iyon. / there’s much more in that house that’s rotten.

wala / nothing
